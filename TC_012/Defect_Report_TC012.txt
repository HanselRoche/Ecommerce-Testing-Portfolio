Summary: Product images map to incorrect 'Dog' placeholder for 'problem_user' account.

Severity: Low (Cosmetic/UI)
Priority: Low

Description:
When logged in as the 'problem_user', all product inventory images fail to load the correct product photography. Instead, every item displays the same placeholder image of a dog, regardless of the product description.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Navigate to www.saucedemo.com.
2. Log in with Username: 'problem_user' and Password: 'secret_sauce'.
3. View the Inventory page.

Expected Result:
Each product should display its specific image (e.g., Backpack should show a backpack).

Actual Result:
All products display identical 'Dog' images.

Environment:
* URL: saucedemo.com
* Browser: Brave Browser
* OS: Windows 11
